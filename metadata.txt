changelog=
        Version 1.0.0:
        - Added the concept of "dagvatten" for tillromr:
            * New table "dagvatten".
            * New column "dagvatten" in tillromr.
            * Trigger in tillromr that updates column dagvatten.
            * Function in the plugin to manually trigger update of column dagvatten.

            The concept of dagvatten is used to calculate and visualize a reduced
            "flode_lPs" when polygons in tillromr overlaps polygons representing areas
            where the rain water is intercepted by areas without infiltration and removed
            from the catchment. An example is dense cities where rainfall is intercepted
            by roads and buildings and lead to surface water.
